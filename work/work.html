MedEasy

MedEasy is an online pharmaceuticals shopping website. The client wanted a light-theme and a vibrant two-tone color scheme, that made the website stand apart from other pharma websites. We also incorporated fresh and vibrant graphics within the website to create an inviting experience. The color-blocking allowed to create a minimal yet engaging experience for users.

Popcorn Time

Popcorn Time is a group movie-watching app concept. The app allows friends and family to create groups and schedule movie sessions with one another. Users can create sessions, invite people, add movies to watch-lists and lots more. The app was designed with a dark-theme that strayed away from the general black color scheme. The icons and graphics are fun and vibrant.

Theming Framework

This theming framework was created using the Angular framework. It allowed users to create their own stylesheets, which consisted only of the various colors that they would like to incorporate in their theme, and effectively use this framework in their applications to change themes without additional overheads. This demo page was created to demonstrate the working of the framework.

ZEP

ZEP is an online recruitment/examination portal concept. The portal allows recruiters to create and host examinations to potential candidates as part of the recruitment process. The portal was designed with a light theme with a pleasant color scheme. The design aimed to remain fresh, friendly and intuitive while also maintaining the formality of an examination portal. Multiple intuitive color combinations were incorporated as well.
